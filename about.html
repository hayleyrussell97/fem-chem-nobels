About The Coders

So who are we... and why do we care about this overly specific topic?

Our names are Hayley and Alex and we work at the Royal Society of Chemistry. We spend our days reading manuscripts about all different areas of Chemistry!

Hayley graduated from The University of Edinburgh in 2019 with a Master's degree in Chemistry with Environmental and Sustainable Chemistry. Alex graduated from the University of Birmingham with a Natural Sciences degree, and then from the University of Cambridge with a Master's degree in Biochemistry in 2018.

Do you know what we noticed during our degrees and during our careers? Chemistry is full of men! And we want to change that!

If you want to get involved or give us some ideas, contact us!
